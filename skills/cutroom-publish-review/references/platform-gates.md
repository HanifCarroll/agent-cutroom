# Platform Gates

## Instagram Reels / TikTok / YouTube Shorts

- Aspect: 9:16, usually 1080x1920.
- Captions: readable on phone, above lower UI controls, not covering the mouth.
- First seconds: visible speaker, claim, motion, or strong visual reason to continue.
- Audio: speech is intelligible on phone speakers; music sits below speech.
- Cover: recognizable at small size; no tiny text.
- Duration: platform-appropriate and not padded.

## LinkedIn

- Aspect: 9:16, 1:1, or 16:9 depending on package.
- Captions: professional and readable; avoid over-styled active-word treatment if it hurts clarity.
- Opening: clear business context or practical claim.
- Post copy: grounded in the transcript or artifact, no invented results.

## Fail Conditions

- Captions overlap platform UI or speaker mouth.
- Active word timing is visibly off.
- Music or effects compete with speech.
- A generated or stock asset has no provenance.
- The render decodes but has black frames, wrong crop, missing audio, or unexpected duration.
